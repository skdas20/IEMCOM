About IEMCOMS

The International Conference on IoT, Electromagnetics, Communication and Sensing (IEMCOMS) is envisioned as a multidisciplinary annual symposium that brings together academia, industry professionals, researchers, and students on a common platform to explore emerging trends in connected technologies and advanced electromagnetic and communication systems. The conference emphasizes affordability and accessibility while maintaining a high standard of scientific excellence, thereby creating an inclusive global forum for knowledge exchange. This conference will also provide an international stage to promote an intense dialogue between academia and industry to bridge the gap between academic research, industry initiatives, and governmental policies. This is fostered through panel discussions, keynotes, invited talks and industry exhibits where academia is exposed to state-of-practice and results from latest experimental works.
